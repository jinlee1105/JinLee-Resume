My name is Jin Lee and I am currently attending the University of California, Riverside. I came from South Korea in 2012, and therefore I am very fluent in both Korean and English. I was always interested in owning my business, and I am very excited to promote my new product. Some of the places I worked in include milk tea shop, watch repair, sushi restaurant, and computer store. I was mostly working there as a marketing manager. To help the owners sell more of their products, I desgined a lot of commercial and event for the customers. For example, I liked to create holiday special discounts because many people go out for shopping and dining on Christmas or Thanksgiving Day. My ultimate goal is to make the certain company prosper so that more people can learn about the good products. Therefore, I always try to promote the products that are really useful to everyone. I think of my project like it is for my family and friends, which means that I care about the quality and guaranteed satisfaction.

This year, I've worked in the milk tea shop for a while and I've learned so much valuable lessons from there. I was able to figure out why people visit the store, what they want to see in the store, when they come to the store mostly, how they want the workers to behave, and what they eventually think about the store. Because everyone is from different culture and background, it is never easy to satisfy everyone. However, I was able to work with my co-workers to find out the demand of consumers and prepare the perfect event and promotion for the store.
